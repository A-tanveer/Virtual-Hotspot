Share Internet Through Hotspot

1. Strat Hotspot with your desired name and password from the application

2. Go to "Control Panel\Network and Internet\Network and Sharing Center ". You'll see a new Network has been created with your given name. Now click on the connection that you want to share with it. In my case it was Ethernet 2.

3. Remember the connection of your hotspots connection name.

4. Go to "Properties> Sharing". Now select "Alolw other network users to connect through this computer's Internet connection" and from the dropdown select your hotspots connection name. In my case it was "Local Area Connection* 4".

5. Apply settings and enjoy sharing your Internet.

Device stuck at connecting

The following procedure works for this issue.

1. Disable your Internet Sharing and enable it again. See left side for instruction.

2. Disconnect from Internet and after a while connect again.
For the most cases this would solve the problem but if it doesn't continue following instructions

3. open 'services.msc' from stat > run > services.msc.

Restart "Internet Connection Sharing (ICS)" service from the "services.msc" window.

4. Open Hotspot application window and stop the hotspot and after a while stat it back.

5. Internet sharing is now enabled and everything gets resolved.


Enjoy sharing Internet.
